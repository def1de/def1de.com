Server Rules


  • No Griefing: Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without permission.
  • Respect Others: Treat all players with kindness and respect. Avoid harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.
  • No Cheating or Hacking: Any use of mods, hacks, or cheats that give unfair advantages is strictly forbidden.
  • No Spamming: Refrain from excessive chat messages, repeated characters, or flooding the chat with irrelevant content.
  • No Advertising: Do not promote other servers, websites, or products in chat.
  • Keep Language Appropriate: Use appropriate language and avoid swearing or offensive language.
  • No Stealing: Respect others' property; stealing from players is not allowed.
  • No PvP without Consent: PvP is only allowed if all parties involved agree to it.
  • No Inappropriate Builds: Avoid building or displaying inappropriate, offensive, or explicit content.
  • No Trolling: Do not intentionally provoke or annoy other players.
  • Respect Staff: Follow the instructions of server staff, and refrain from arguing or disrespecting them.
  • No X-Ray Texture Packs or Mods: Using X-ray texture packs or mods to see through blocks is prohibited.
  • No Spawn Camping: Avoid camping or killing players near spawn points.
  • Report Issues Properly: If you encounter a problem or a player breaking the rules, report it to staff privately.
  • No Impersonation: Do not pretend to be someone else, especially staff members or server owners.
  • No Lag Machines: Creating machines or structures to cause server lag or crashes is not allowed.
  • Build Responsibly: Avoid large, resource-heavy builds that may impact server performance.
  • No Abusing Glitches: Report any glitches or exploits rather than abusing them.
  • Have Fun: Remember that the primary goal is to enjoy the game and create a friendly community.
